About Paul Sweeney

Graduated in Agriculture at University of Wales, Aberystwyth.

Agricultural Adviser with ADAS in Lancashire & Leicestershire.

Technical Agronomist with Kemira Agro from 1990-97

Established on-farm agronomy business in 1998, to form Independent Agronomy Northwest.

Specialising in cereals, OSR, beans, maize, potatoes and grassland agronomy.

Expert in Crop Protection, Soil Management & Crop Nutrition.

Detailed advice on Cross Compliance & NVZ Regulations.

Undertakes specialist contracts such as farm business advice, technical training, land contamination assessment, crop complaints & expert witness.

Professional Member of AICC, BASIS & FACTS.
